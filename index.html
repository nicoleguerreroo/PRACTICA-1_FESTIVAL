WELCOME

More than 400.000 People of Tomorrow from all corners of the world have united in Boom, during two legendary weekends filled with love, unity, and unforgettable moments, enjoying breathtaking performances. A cruel twist of fate, the fire that broke out on the Mainstage during the final stages of construction and rehearsals, made People of Tomorrow around the planet hold their breath in anxious apprehension, but this one will be a memory forever. Together we created a story like no other.

EXPERIENCES

The Tomorrowland Immersive Experience in Madrid has come to an end. The magical journey at the Movistar Arena in Madrid has officially closed its doors. A heartfelt thank you to everyone who stepped into the world of Tomorrowland and explored its iconic themes in this one-of-a-kind immersive experience.

Stay tuned, more information about future locations will be announced soon. Keep an eye on the website and Tomorrowland’s official channels for updates.
